# UVA 10170 - The Hotel with Infinite Rooms

## 題目描述

The city of Halua has a strange hotel with infinite rooms. The rooms are numbered 1, 2, 3, 4, ... and so on. But the problem is that the hotel has a strange way of accommodating guests. The first guest is assigned to room S. Then the next guest is assigned to room S+1, and so on. But when the number of guests assigned to a room reaches the room number, the next guest is assigned to the next room.

## 輸入說明

The input contains several lines. Each line contains two integers S and D (1 <= S, D <= 10^14).

## 輸出說明

For each line of input, print the room number where the D-th guest is assigned.

## 範例輸入

```
1 1
1 2
1 3
1 4
1 5
1 6
1 7
1 8
1 9
1 10
```

## 範例輸出

```
1
2
2
3
3
3
4
4
4
4
```
